Reimagining Air Travel Through Modular Flight

We’re revolutionizing air travel by separating takeoff, landing, and cruising into specialized, reusable modules.

In traditional aviation, each aircraft carries heavy engines and landing gear through every phase of the flight, even though they're only critical for takeoff and landing. We break that cycle. Our dedicated launch and recovery drones operate continuously, serving multiple passenger hulls throughout the day. This cuts down on idle equipment, spreads capital costs across more flights, and ultimately makes travel more affordable.

Why This Matters

By decoupling takeoff and landing from the passenger hull, airlines can scale capacity efficiently. The hulls become simpler and cheaper, while the reusable drones stay busy all day. This approach spreads costs over many more flights, reduces ticket prices, and makes air travel more accessible and sustainable for everyone.
